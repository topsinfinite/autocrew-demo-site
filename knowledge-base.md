## Patient Portal / Health Assistant

Harmony Family Health offers an AI-powered Health Assistant that gives patients instant access to their health records. The Health Assistant is available 24/7 and is voice-enabled.

### Health Assistant Features

- **Medication List:** View current medications, dosages, and refill schedules
- **Appointments:** Check upcoming visits and request new appointments
- **Lab Results:** Access latest lab work and test results
- **24/7 Chat Support:** Get answers to health questions anytime
- **Voice-Enabled:** Speak naturally — the assistant understands natural language
- **HIPAA Compliant:** Patient health data is always secure and private

### Health Assistant Suggested Actions
- "What medications am I currently taking?"
- "When is my next appointment?"
- "Show me my recent lab results"

### Health Assistant Welcome Message
"Hello! I can help you check your health records, medications, and appointments. How can I assist you today?"

### Example Health Assistant Interactions

**Patient:** What medications am I currently taking?
**Assistant:** Based on your records, you're currently prescribed:
- Metformin 500mg - twice daily
- Lisinopril 10mg - once daily
- Vitamin D3 2000 IU - once daily
Would you like me to check for any refills due?

**Patient:** When is my next appointment?
**Assistant:** Your next appointment is: March 15, 2026 at 10:30 AM with Dr. Sarah Chen - Annual Wellness Visit
